= link:ntp.html[NTP] / Time Servers

通常指向 `pool.ntp.org` 即可，它背後會透過 DNS 分散流量。

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
$ ping -c 1 pool.ntp.org
PING pool.ntp.org (59.125.147.68) 56(84) bytes of data.
...
$ ping -c 1 pool.ntp.org
PING pool.ntp.org (123.204.45.116) 56(84) bytes of data.
...
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
