= Android Networking =

== 工具 ==

 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mankind.ipconfig[ipconfig] - 可以列出詳細的網路資訊
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ulfdittmer.android.ping[Ping & DNS]

== 監測網路活動或統計流量 ==

 * tcpdump for Android?
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=lv.n3o.shark[Shark for Root]
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=lv.n3o.sharkreader[Shark Reader]
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.carl.trafficcounter[Traffic Counter Extended] - 可以看個別 App 的流量
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.rgruet.android.g3watchdog[3G Watchdog]
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mobileCounter&feature=related_apps[Mobile Counter]
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.onavo.android.onavoid&feature=related_apps[Onavo Count] - App Watch 可以看個別 App 的流量，有些系統層級的應用會被計入 "Android OS + others"。
 * https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mobidia.android.mdm&feature=related_apps[My Data Manager]
