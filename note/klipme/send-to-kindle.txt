________________________________________________________________________________
"Send to Kindle" is a Browser extension for Kindle owners who prefer reading web content on their devices. It’s designed to offer a quick way for pushing web content to Kindle, then you can read the articles or news later on your device.
________________________________________________________________________________

目前提供 https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/send-to-kindle-by-klipme/ipkfnchcgalnafehpglfbommidgmalan[Chrome] 跟 Safari 的 extensions，其他的瀏覽器則透過 bookmarklet 支援。

安裝後要先完成兩個設定：（按下 bookmarklet 會出現設定畫面）

 * 設定 Send-to-Kindle E-mail Address
 * 到 https://www.amazon.com/gp/digital/fiona/manage#pdocSettings[Manage Your Kindle > Personal Document Settings] 將 `kindle@klip.me` 加入 Approved Personal Document E-mail List。

之後按下 "K" 按鈕或 bookmarklet，就會出現目前頁面或選取範圍的預覽畫面（相對易讀的版面，但表格的效果很糟！），按下 Send 就會透過 Send-to-Kindle E-mail Address 送到 Kindle 設備上。
