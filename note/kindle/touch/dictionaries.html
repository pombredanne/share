KT / Dictionaries

Kindle Touch 內建兩本牛津字典－The New Oxford American Dictionary 跟 Oxford Dictionary of English。

預設的字典可以在 Menu > Settings > Device Options > Language and Dictionaries > Dictionaries (Set the default dictionary for each language) > English 替換。

網路上很多人推薦 Collins Cobuild Advanced Learner’s Dictionary of British English，把 .mobi 放到 dictionaries 放到 dictionaries 子目錄，再設定 default dictionary 即可。

注意到了嗎？我們設定的是 English 的預設字典。製作電子書要注意語言的設定，否則字典會起不了作用：

Dictionary Not Found

There are no dictionaries on your device for this book’s language. To download a dictionary, go to Archived Items or purchase one from the Kindle Store.
