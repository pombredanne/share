:title: [OpenNI] 坐標系統

 * Projective Coordinates - a two-dimentional map of depth values （為什麼 convert method 接受／回傳 Point3D？）
 * Real World Coordinates - A collection of three-dimentional points, in millimeters
   ** 從 sensor 的角度出發，右手邊是 +x，上面是 +y，往前是 +z。Loc 2350 Figure 7-2 有張圖很清楚
 * Some data in OpenNI is returned in projective coordinates (for example, depth maps), and some is returned in real-world coordinates (for example, skeleton joints). Pay attention when comparing the two.
 * translation can be made between the two systems. `DepthGenerator.convertProjectiveToRealWorld(point3D)`, `DepthGenerator.convertRealWorldToProjective(point3D)`
 * `org.OpenNI.Point3D` - `getX()`, `getY()`, `getZ()` (`float`)
