Diigo - Collect and Highlight, Then Remember

Diigo 提供了很多工具，可以用來提昇自己網站跟 Diigo 之間的黏性：

  • Linkrolls

  • Add to Diigo

－些使用上的經驗：

  • 閱讀過後（加註 highlights 跟 comments），別忘了在記憶最鮮明的時候，將對該份文件整體的心得寫在 bookmark 的 description 裡。雖然日後還是可以從 annotations "慢慢" 找回當初的感覺，但如果當時有把它寫下來，就可以一眼看出文件的重點。

令人振奮的消息 (2012-12-24)

If you continue to annotate the page, these annotations will be merged. Annotations are saved to our servers permanently even if the URL changes. But they cannot be displayed on the original page.We will support to save cached cached pages inserted with annotations in Diigo V6. Making annotations directly on cached pages will also be available with that version. Please stay tuned.
