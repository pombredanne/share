:title: [Eng] Scenarios: Presentation

"做簡報" 本身有兩種意思，搭配的動詞不同。對某人做簡報用 'give'，製作簡報則用 'make'，例如：

 * Giving a successful oral presentation.
 * Making a great presentation.

== 慣用語 ==

 * I'll let partner to introduce the remaining...
 * as you can see, ...
