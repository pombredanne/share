:title: [Ruby] Getting Started

首先確認 Ruby 1.9.x 已經link:install.html[安裝]妥當。

[source,console]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
$ ruby --version
ruby 1.9.3p0 (2011-10-30 revision 33570) [x86_64-linux]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[source,ruby]
.`hello.rb`
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
# say hello to such a great gem.
puts "Hello, Ruby!";
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

結尾的分號通常不會加，除非要把多個 statements 寫在同一行。

[source,console]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
$ ruby hello.rb
Hello, Ruby!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[source,ruby]
.`hello.rb`
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
#!/usr/bin/env ruby
# say hello to such a great gem.
puts "Hello, Ruby!";
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[source,console]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
chmod a+x hello.rb
./hello.rb
Hello, Ruby!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
