:title: [Chrome][Extension] 語言學習

 * https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/pronounce/ppfjcendbbcnfhaimddfmjchjkepaman[Pronounce]
 * https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/google-dictionary-by-goog/mgijmajocgfcbeboacabfgobmjgjcoja[Google Dictionary (by Google)] - 點兩下選取單字就會跳出解釋以及發音的連結。
 * https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/select-and-speak/gfjopfpjmkcfgjpogepmdjmcnihfpokn[Select and Speak] - 選取文字後按 Ctrl + Shift + S 就可以用 "真人語音" 唸出文字，效果很不錯。可以控制音量、男女聲、速度等。
